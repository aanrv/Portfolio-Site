Neighbor Tiles - Details


Description

Neighbor Tiles is a puzzle game for iOS.

The game consists of a grid of tiles. Each tile is either one of two different colors (white and black by default). Tapping on a tile inverts its color, along with the color of its neighboring tiles. To solve the puzzle, the player must make all the tiles black.

The game consists of two different modes: Standard mode and Time Challenge mode.

  • Standard mode consists of a series of puzzles. After solving each puzzle, the player unlocks the next level.
  • Time Challenge mode throws a series of randomly generated puzzles at the user. The puzzles increase in difficulty over time. After solving each puzzle, the player gets a few additional seconds on the timer. The game is over when the timer runs out.

The player also has a few hints. These hints show how to solve a puzzle, but they are available in limited quantity.
